What is PagesLike?

PagesLike is a new way to get exposure for your site across a community of web sites that share a common readership. You can attract more readers to your site, and help your readers find relevant related content by joining a community of other web sites like yours.

For example, TechFlash is the anchor site for startup and technology news from the Seattle area. But there are many smaller company blogs and vertical new sites in our community. All of these sites can easily cross-promote between each other by joining the Seattle Technology PagesLike community.

How does it work?

Join one of our community sites to find the most appropriate group for you.

Once you embed the PagesLike widget, your readers will be able to see the most popular related stories from other web sites in that community. Once your site has been approved by your community manager, links to your site will also appear on other web sites in the community.

There is no additional work for you to do to submit articles to PagesLike. We automatically recognize new stories from your site whenever the widget is displayed there. We then compare the content and keywords that your story uses, and propose to your readers the most popular recent articles from other sites in your community. Once your site has been approved to submit content in your community, your site will also be recommended to readers on other sites, when they are looking at articles that are similar to ones on your site.

Try it Now!

Step 1: Embed our code snippet in your web page (usually in a side-bar navigation area):

<script src="http://www.pageslike.com/widget.js"></script>
<script>
  global_namespace.Import('pageslike.widget').InsertWidget({community:'general'});
</script>

Step 2: There is no Step 2 - that's all there is to it!
